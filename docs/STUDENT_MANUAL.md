# Student Manual — Smart Bus Tracking System

**Institution:** Narayana Engineering College

## 1. Sign Up / Sign In
1. Open the app URL and click **Sign Up**.
2. Enter name, roll no., department, phone, email, password.
3. Verify your email (link sent to inbox).
4. Sign in.

## 2. Home
- **My Route:** shows the bus assigned to your route.
- **Live Map:** real-time bus location with ETA.
- **Stops:** upcoming stop list with estimated arrival.
- **Announcements:** notices from admin.

## 3. Live Tracking
- Green dot = bus running.
- Red dot = delayed.
- Grey = trip not started.
- Map falls back to OpenStreetMap if Google Maps is unavailable — no action needed.

## 4. Notifications
- Bus approaching your stop (geofence 60 m).
- Bus departed.
- Announcements.

## 5. Profile
Update phone, password, and department in **Profile**.

## 6. Offline
If you lose internet, an offline banner appears. The last known bus location remains visible; live updates resume automatically.
