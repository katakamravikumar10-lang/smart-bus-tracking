# Faculty Manual — Smart Bus Tracking System

**Institution:** Narayana Engineering College

## 1. Access
Sign up with your faculty email and department. Faculty role is auto-assigned.

## 2. Features
- Live bus tracking for staff routes.
- ETA to campus / home stop.
- Announcements from administration.
- Profile management.

## 3. Notifications
Receive alerts on bus arrival, departure, delays, and college-wide announcements.

## 4. Reporting an Issue
Use the **Feedback** option in your profile menu or email `transport@narayanaengg.edu.in`.

## 5. Support
`support@narayanaengg.edu.in`
